What you build & own

Every part of the system is an asset that stays with you. Not a login you rent — a machine your team runs, extends, and keeps. Talent teams don't need more AI-themed karaoke machines. They need real infrastructure. Here's what's in it.

AGENTS
Sourcing agents
Continuously surface off-market, signal-matched candidates for your roles. The pull never stops and never leaves with a vendor.
ENGAGEMENT
Candidate sequencers
Personalized, multi-channel sequences (email · LinkedIn · SMS), built around follow-ups — where 82% of replies actually come from.
DATA
Intent-signal data layer
Tenure, promotions, team changes and more, in a store you own. Your data never trains someone else's AI to replace you.
PIPELINE
Live sourcing pipeline & dashboard
Full visibility into every candidate, signal, and message sent — the reporting layer stays yours, not the vendor's.
OPERATING SYSTEM
The workflows that make it work
The prompts, playbooks, and workflow design that turn tools into ROI. This is the part teams stall on alone — and the part we build with you.
YOUR TEAM
The capability, in-house
Your recruiters learn to run and extend the system. The skill lives inside your team instead of inside a retainer.
// open by design
Open APIs. Open architecture. Natural-language workflows. Multi-agent systems. Real portability. Real control. That's the spec for everything we build with you — no black box, nothing trapped inside someone else's platform.
// the ownership contract
If we ever part ways, you keep all of it — the agents, the sequences, the data, the dashboard, the playbooks. That's the entire point. An agency rents you effort and walks off with the relationship and the data. We build you the capability and leave you the asset.
