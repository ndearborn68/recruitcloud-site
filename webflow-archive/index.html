Frequently asked questions

How is this different from a typical recruiting agency?

Traditional agencies charge 15-30% of first-year salary and use the same LinkedIn searches and job ads you could do yourself. We use AI to track data signals that most recruiters miss completely, scanning over 200 million profiles to spot top candidates before they’ve even left their current role.  Then we send hyper-personalized outreach at scale to engage them and get them interested in your role. Our team screens everyone first, so you only interview people who are genuinely interested and qualified. The result is better candidates in half the time at a fraction of the cost.

Can I just hire talent myself?

You can, but why would you want to? We have 30 years of recruiting experience combined with AI technology that accesses 200 million profiles. We do this faster and at a fraction of what agencies charge because it's all we do. Your time should be spent on what matters most: creating an amazing employee experience and selecting the best candidates from the qualified pool we deliver to you. We handle everything for less than you'd spend doing it yourself.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients have their first interviews booked within a week of starting with us. We map your ideal talent in 2-3 days, then you'll see your first qualified candidates in 3-4 days. Fresh candidates flow in weekly after that initial batch.

We already have in-house recruiters. Why would we need you?

Your recruiters are valuable, and we make them more powerful rather than replacing them. We handle the time-consuming sourcing and initial screening, using AI data analysis and outreach tools. This frees your team up to focus on interviews and final hiring decisions.

Will this work for hard-to-fill technical roles?

This is actually where we shine brightest. We use AI to find technical talent across 200 million profiles, tracking career progression signals and skill development patterns that identify specialists others miss completely. We've successfully placed senior engineers, product managers, data scientists, and C-level leaders across media, technology, marketing, finance and more. If the role exists and requires specific technical skills, we can find the right person for it.

Does this improve our internal recruitment ops?

We automate the tasks that bog down recruiting teams. Our system keeps your contact data fresh, adds new contacts automatically, and tracks job changes across your CRM and ATS. You never work with stale data that kills your outreach or makes your candidate lists useless. Your recruiters focus on building relationships and making hires while we handle all the data work that usually slows everything down.
