Pricing

You're not paying per shortlist. You're paying once to build a system you keep — then run at internal cost instead of agency rates.

For reference, the spend this replaces: an agency placement runs $15,000–$35,000 per hire (SHRM); RPO runs ~$200k–$500k for 50 hires a year. Internal cost-per-hire averages ~$4,700. The system you own moves you toward the bottom of that range — and you stop paying the markup every time.
